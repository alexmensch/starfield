Welcome to Starfield

Starfield is an interactive 3D map of — real stars. Each star is rendered at the colour and apparent brightness you would actually see from the camera's current position.

Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, and hover over a star to see its name. Click a star to focus on it, click a second star to measure the distance between them, then click the far end of the line to travel there. Search for “Sol” to return home to our sun. Enjoy!
